Retail Customer Support FAQ

For account help, customers can reset passwords from the sign-in page, update contact information in account settings, and manage communication preferences online. Digital coupons must be clipped before checkout and are linked to the rewards account used for the transaction. Store availability shown online is an estimate and can change because inventory moves quickly. For product recalls, customers should stop using the affected item and follow posted recall instructions. Customer support can help with order issues, refunds, membership questions, delivery concerns, coupon troubleshooting, and store information. For urgent food safety concerns, contact the store directly or visit the customer service desk. Keep receipts, order numbers, and product packaging when possible to speed up support.
