Delivery and Pickup Policy FAQ

Customers can place delivery or pickup orders through the website or mobile app by selecting a store, choosing a time window, and adding eligible products to the cart. Pickup orders are brought to a designated pickup area after the customer checks in. Delivery orders are delivered to the address selected during checkout. Availability, fees, substitutions, minimum order amounts, and time windows vary by location. Customers can allow substitutions, reject substitutions, or add item notes. If an item is unavailable, the shopper may provide an approved substitute or remove the item from the order. Temperature-sensitive items are handled with care, but customers should retrieve pickup orders promptly. Missing, damaged, or incorrect delivery items can be reported through order help for refund or replacement review.
