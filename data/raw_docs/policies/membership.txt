Membership Benefits FAQ

Retail Rewards members receive personalized digital coupons, weekly member-only prices, birthday offers, fuel or grocery rewards when available, faster checkout with saved preferences, and access to online purchase history for easier returns. Members can clip coupons in the mobile app or website before shopping, then use their phone number, loyalty ID, or app barcode at checkout. Membership is free for the standard rewards program. Some premium delivery subscriptions may include unlimited eligible delivery orders, reduced service fees, and special savings windows. Benefits vary by store, market, and promotion. Customers should confirm clipped offers before checkout because some discounts require minimum purchases, participating products, or expiration dates.
