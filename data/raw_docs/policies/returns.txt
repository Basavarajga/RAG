Return Policy and Refund FAQ

Customers may return most unopened grocery, household, health, beauty, and general merchandise items within 30 days of purchase when they have a receipt, digital order confirmation, or membership purchase history. Fresh produce, meat, seafood, bakery, deli, and floral items are covered by our freshness promise: if the item is spoiled, damaged, missing, or not as expected, customers should contact customer support or visit the service desk as soon as possible for a replacement, store credit, or refund. Alcohol, tobacco, gift cards, lottery, pharmacy prescriptions, and regulated products may be non-returnable or may require additional verification according to local law. Online delivery or pickup refunds can be requested through order help. Refunds are generally returned to the original payment method; store credit may be offered when proof of purchase is not available.
